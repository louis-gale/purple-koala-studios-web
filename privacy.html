Privacy Policy for Purple Koala Studios

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Purple Koala Studios! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you use our mobile games. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what we do with your information.

2. What Data We Collect

Information You Provide: If you contact us for support, we collect your email address and the content of your inquiry to resolve your issue.

Information Collected Automatically: When you play our games, we automatically collect:

Identifiers: A randomly generated user ID to save your game progress.

Technical Data: Device model, operating system, and language settings to ensure our games run smoothly on your device.

Gameplay Data: Information about your interaction with the game, such as progress, achievements, and play time.

Third-Party Data: We do not collect payment information. All in-app purchases are handled securely by Google Play or the App Store.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for essential purposes:

Service Provision: To save your game progress and ensure the game functions correctly.

Customer Support: To respond to your feedback and support requests.

Improvement: To analyze game performance (e.g., identifying crashes) so we can make our games more stable.

4. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted partners who help us run our business:

Technical Partners: We use services like Firebase (for cloud messaging) or analytics tools (e.g., Bugsnag) to monitor app health.

Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with legal processes or government requests.

5. Data Security & Retention

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. We only keep your data for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to your data, request deletion, or object to certain processing activities. You can contact us at any time to exercise these rights, including requesting the permanent deletion of your account and any associated data, by emailing us at contact@purplekoalastudios.com.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through an in-game notice or an update to this website.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out to us at:

Email: contact@purplekoalastudios.com
